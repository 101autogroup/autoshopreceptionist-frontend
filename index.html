Questions auto shop owners ask us

If you're used to doing everything yourself, letting an AI pick up your phones can feel like a big step. Here's what other shop owners wanted to know before signing up.

Will my customers know they're talking to AI?

Most customers simply hear a clear, helpful voice that answers quickly and books their appointment. The AI is trained to sound natural and professional. We don't hide that it's AI, but the experience is so smooth most callers don't ask.

Can the AI handle complex diagnostic questions?

The agent is designed to handle common concerns, capture symptoms, and then guide the caller to an appointment so your techs can diagnose in person. It won't guess at what's wrong — it'll book the job correctly.

How do you connect to my phone system?

During onboarding, we review whether you use a VoIP provider, call forwarding, or a traditional line. In most cases, we forward your main number (or after-hours number) to the AI line so that calls are answered without hardware changes. We'll walk you through this step-by-step during your demo.

What if I get a lot of spam calls or price shoppers?

The AI can screen out obvious spam, collect basic info from price shoppers, and focus on converting real repair opportunities into appointments. You control how aggressive or selective the agent is in booking jobs.

Is there a long-term contract?

No. Plans are month-to-month. If Auto Shop Receptionist isn't adding value, you can cancel anytime. Our goal is to earn your business every single month by keeping your bays full.

Can you support bilingual callers?

Yes. Many shops serve communities where more than one language is common. We can configure the agent to greet in one language and offer another, or detect and respond appropriately based on the caller's preference.
